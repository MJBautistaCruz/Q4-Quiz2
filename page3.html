Baguio

Hailed as the Summer Capital of the Philippines, Baguio is the top destination to go to if you want to take a break from the tropical heat in the lowlands.


Bohol

The home of the famous Chocolate Hills, Bohol is one of the most visited destinations in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. T he island province offers breathtaking spots for history buffs, beach lovers, and adrenaline junkies.


Siargao

Siargao is a province in Surigao del Norte that consists of nine towns on an island. Siargao, dubbed the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines," is primarily to blame for the spread of surfing throughout the nation.
